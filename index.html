Jamie Kee

Researcher · Techno-economic Analysis

National Laboratory of the Rockies  ·  Golden, CO

Jamie Kee is a researcher at NLR. His work typically focuses on techno-economic analysis of hydrogen production, transmission, distribution, storage, and end use. Kee's research uses physics-based modeling tools and generally accepted accounting principles financial analysis to evaluate and analyze technologies along the hydrogen value chain.
